Take A Look Into David Dobriks Girlfriend List

August 2024 · 4 minute read

The Slovakian Prince, who is ruling over the world of YouTube, David Dobrik, was born on July 23, 1996, in Košice, Slovakia. He armed his popularity since the Vine days and later entered the world of YouTube in late 2014. Since then, David’s popularity is skyrocketing every day. The cute David shares unfiltered vlogs in his YouTube channel, maybe the reason why people are so attracted to his uploads.

Apart from being a YouTube Star, David is a kind and humble person who does anything for his squad, The Vlog Squad. So, what is the love life of charming David? Who is he dating? Let’s know more.

The YouTube Prince

With around 15.5M subscribers on YouTube, David Dobrik has massed an enormous following. He uploads funny prank videos and collaborates with other fellow YouTubers and celebrities as well. After the great YouTube success, David Dobrik has become a judge of America’s Most Musical Family and co-starred in movies like Airplane Mode, The Angry Birds Movie 2, and FML.

The cute heartthrob David has been quite open about his romantic relationships. Here are a few beautiful women who were linked with adorable David.

1) Lorraine Nash

The four-minute and twenty seconds video posted by David blew the trending lists, as it took all his fans by shook. The sexy heartthrob, David, got married to his best friend Jason Nash’s mother, Lorraine Nash. David initiated as a prank after getting a sarcastic satire by Jason. When David Dobrik declared that Jason’s kids liked his ex-wife’s boyfriend, Jason replied with the note that David would never have an ex-wife as no one would even marry him.

After hearing such a satire, David flew to Jason’s mom’s house and proposed her on the spot, and both David and Lorraine kept on laughing hysterically.

However, the couple divorced in June 2019, after a month of their marriage. David confirmed the split through a tweet.

We can see that David was successful in being his best friends’ stepdad and the husband of a lovely woman.

2) Liza Koshy

David Dobrik and Liza Koshy were ‘The YouTube’ couple. All the Vlog Squad fans loved the chemistry they had with each other. With Liza being the funniest one in the pair, David added even more of his jokes, and the harmony kept on getting better and better.

However, the couple broke up in 2018, and the fans got to know about it only after when David posted a video titled ‘we broke up.’  It took the internet by storm, and fans expressed their sadness.

As for now, even after the breakup, David and Liza seem to be best friends and appear on each other’s YouTube videos as well.

3) Natalie Mariduena (rumored)

Natalie Mariduena or Natalina Noel is the childhood best friend of David Dobrik and an assistant as well. As Natalie is always present in David’s vlog and exploring the world with him, it is no wonder that fans keep speculating that there is something between David and Natalie.
